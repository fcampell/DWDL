# **Facade Ad Revenue: Zürich (EGID-based Potential & DCF)**

> **Goal:** Estimate the *daily view potential* of building facades in the City of Zürich and translate it into *advertising revenue potential*.  
> Incorporate a **Discounted Cash Flow (DCF)** model to account for permitting periods, installation CAPEX, and projected cash inflows.

---

## **1. Real Estate x Advertising Opportunity**

### **Real Estate Perspective (Supply Side)**
- Institutional and private **real estate owners** continuously seek new ways to enhance their **asset revenue performance** beyond rent.  
- Many buildings in Zürich have **unused facade areas** suitable for physical or digital advertisements.  
- These surfaces represent **latent assets** that can be activated through data-driven visibility and profitability assessments.

### **Advertising Perspective (Demand Side)**
- Despite the digital transformation, **Out-of-Home (OOH)** advertising remains a resilient and high-value medium.  
- Advertisers require **quantifiable exposure metrics** — comparable to *digital impressions* — to justify investments in physical ad spaces.  
- Premium urban locations with **high pedestrian and traffic visibility** drive higher advertising ROI.

---

## **2. Problem Statement**

There is **no integrated solution** in Zürich that:
- Quantifies the **visibility (views)** of individual building facades based on location, orientation, and surrounding traffic/pedestrian flows.  
- Converts this data into **monetary advertising potential**.  
(- Provides a **financial feasibility model** that integrates CAPEX, permitting, and long-term cash flow forecasting.  )

This gap limits real estate owners’ ability to **monetize their facade assets** and **benchmark investment opportunities**.

---

## **3. Solution Overview**

### **Core Concept**
A data-driven **SaaS platform** and **per-building analytics service** that enable asset owners and managers to:
1. Identify facades with high potential advertising value.
2. Estimate expected daily **view counts** per facade (derived from traffic and pedestrian data).
3. Translate visibility metrics into **estimated advertising revenue**.
4. Model **DCF-based investment performance** accounting for approval timelines, CAPEX, and revenue projections.

---

## **4. Product Offering**

- **Dashboard (SaaS):**  
  Subscription-based platform licensed by real estate portfolio managers and asset managers.  
  Provides portfolio-wide analytics and benchmarking of facade potential.

- **Per-Building Reports:**  
  Individual analytic reports (one-time purchase) for single buildings or development projects, including visibility assessment and DCF results.

---

## **5. Dashboard Features & UI/UX**

- **Input:** User enters an **EGID** (Swiss Building ID) or **address**.  
- **Output:**  
  - Estimated **daily potential views** per facade (traffic + pedestrian visibility).  
  - **Monetization model** translating view potential into annual advertising revenue.  
  - **Portfolio-wide analytics** to compare return potential across assets.  
  - **DCF simulation module** projecting investment performance over a planning horizon.  
    - Includes installation and permitting CAPEX.  
    - Highlights payback period, IRR, and NPV impacts on asset valuation.

---

## **6. Business Impact**

- **For Real Estate Owners:** Unlock a new revenue stream by commercializing underutilized facade assets.  
- **For Asset Managers:** Integrate facade potential into portfolio-level valuation and investment decisions.  
- **For Advertisers:** Access verified, data-backed locations with predictable exposure and financial transparency.
